Applus Energie

Applus Energie is a project aimed at the creation of a system for monitoring energy consumption and climate quality. It is a project co-founded by Regione Autonoma Valle d'Aosta within the context of Alcotra Innovazione.

Two monitoring systems were implemented during this project in two different buildings of Politecnico di Torino: the first one located in Verrès (AO) and the second located in Mirafiori (Cittadella del Design e della Mobilità) of Politecnico di Torino. The main goal is to show to the students their consumption in a clear manner, creating a greater awareness.

In the context of Applus Energie, some Living Labs were leaded in order to share proposals or ideas during the phase of the interface designing.

Proxima Centauri developed the system in collaboration with the Elite research group of Politecnico di Torino.
